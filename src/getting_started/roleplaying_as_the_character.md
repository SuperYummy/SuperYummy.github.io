## Roleplaying as the Character
Now that you own Hajime Hinata, you can begin roleplaying

1. Enter the command **/roleplay**

    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SuperYummy/SuperYummy.github.io/refs/heads/main/images/Roleplaying%20as%20the%20Character/1.png"/>

    *The user entering /roleplay*

2. Select **Hajime Hinata**

    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SuperYummy/SuperYummy.github.io/refs/heads/main/images/Roleplaying%20as%20the%20Character/2.png"/>

    *The user's characters, showing Hajime Hinata*

3. Press **Play**

    <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SuperYummy/SuperYummy.github.io/refs/heads/main/images/Roleplaying%20as%20the%20Character/3.png"/>

    *Hajime Hinata with the Play button*

That's it! You are now playing as Hajime Hinata

Right now, your world does not have interactive elements yet. There isn't much to do besides existing in the Beach. If you want to stop playing, press the Menu button and select Stop Playing
